## Animation Specifications

### Page Load Animations

- Duration: 0.6-0.8 seconds
- Easing: "easeOut"
- Stagger: 0.1-0.2 seconds

### Scroll Animations

- Threshold: 0.1 (trigger when 10% visible)
- Duration: 0.5-0.6 seconds
- Easing: "easeOut"

### Hover Effects

- Scale: 1.02-1.05
- Duration: 0.2-0.3 seconds
- Easing: "easeInOut"
